About Us

A Legacy of Learning
Excellence & Values

Central Public School stands as a beacon of quality education in the heart of Saunha Ramkola, Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh. Founded with the singular mission of making world-class education accessible to every child, we nurture curious minds from Nursery through Class 8.

Our institution blends rigorous academics with character-building, sports, arts, and life skills — creating well-rounded individuals ready to thrive in tomorrow's world. With a sprawling, secure campus and state-of-the-art infrastructure, we provide every child the environment they deserve to flourish.

CBSE-aligned curriculum with local cultural integration
English-medium instruction with Hindi fluency focus
Activity-based learning in smart classrooms
Regular parent-teacher communication
